Document Guide

This appraisal document is designed to increase the fairness in our appraisal process.

It is expected that every team lead sets KPIs for their team members at the beginning of every appraisal cycle which forms the basis for the work done during the period.

📊
Section 1 — KPI Review

This contains the KPIs for each team member. KPIs per objective should be between 2–4. Some terms explained below:

Functional Objectives
This is related to your departmental objectives.
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
A quantifiable measure of performance over a period of time.
Weights
Assigns the level of importance of each KPI to the overall outcome. Total must be 100%.
😊
Section 2 — Values & Behaviours

This section measures critical aspects of BCM's culture and values. Six fixed behaviours are rated by you and your manager.
